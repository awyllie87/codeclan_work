count_data <- students %>% 
  group_by(superpower, school_year) %>% 
  summarise(count = n())

ggplot(count_data) +
  aes(x = superpower, y = count, fill = school_year) +
  geom_bar(stat = "identity")

Make all the points blue. For geom_bar, the colour of the bar is controlled by fill, but for geom_point the colour of the points are controlled by colour. Make the colour of the points depend on the superpower the student wishes they had. Write down what the graph tells you overall.
